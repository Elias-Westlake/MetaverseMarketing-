What is the Metaverse?


Commonly referred to as a “digital universe”, the Metaverse merges reality and the virtual world. The Metaverse is a shared digital space that utilizes virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) technologies to immerse users into the digital world. As the Metaverse continues to grow, it’s importance as a marketing tool continues to grow with it. The Metaverse is not just a trend, and is quickly becoming one of the biggest digital marketing opportunities of current times. Marketers can integrate Metaverse marketing into their strategies, and align them with real-world experiences to empower their campaigns.
